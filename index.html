School Hostel

Sanjude Boy’s Hostel

Citadel boys’ hostel is run by the fathers of Diocese of Kanjirapally. We admit students from Class VI onwards in the hostel. Special attention is given for self-learning. The hostel is provided with all modern amenities. The rooms are furnished with separate beds, cupboards and study table for each student. A separate study hall is also provided for the self-study and preparation. There is a dedicated housekeeping department which ensures the cleanliness of the hostel. The school library provides internet facilities to the hostel students for academic purposes. Telephone and laundry services are also available.

  • New School Computer Lab

    Our Computer Lab is fully equipped with sufficient hardware and software. Computer Science is taught in classes I to X in lieu of work experience. NIIT also imparts certified IT education for Classes I to X which has been designed with latest topics.
